The Overlooked Issue of Athletic Mental Health


To me, this is extremely essential, as athletes' mental health is often overlooked. As an athlete who struggles with mental health myself, I understand how detrimental being overlooked and unsupported can feel for athletes struggling like me. Athletics can become very consuming and difficult to balance with the other demands of life, which I speak about from personal experience. With the mention of and awareness of this issue from athletes like me, I am very hopeful for the future of growth between athletes and their mental health.
