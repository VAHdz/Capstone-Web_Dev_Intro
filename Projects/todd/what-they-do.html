The Athletes Mental Health Foundation's Mission

At the Athletes Mental Health Foundation - Athletes will be properly supported. From resources such as mental health professional therapists and free trainings, to discussions and ongoing research projects, they make it easy for athletes to feel supported and heard. To me, this is extremely essential, as athletes' mental health is often overlooked. As an athlete who struggles with mental health myself, I understand how detrimental being overlooked and unsupported can feel for athletes struggling like me; and the Athletes Mental Health Foundation is all about bridging that gap.
