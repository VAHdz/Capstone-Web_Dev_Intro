About the Capstone

  • Description: This capstone project introduces students to the fundamentals of web development through hands-on work in HTML and CSS. Students create a research-based site focused on local charities connected to a cause of their choice. Along the way, they explore AI tools, events such as hackathons, and real-world applications of computer science. By the end of the course, each student complete one meaningful projects and gains practical, showcase-ready skills.
